Description

Ballooning events were contested at the 1900 Universale Exposition alongside the Olympic Games, so in some record books one will see ballooning events considered part of the 1900 Olympics. Ballooning has never appeared at any other Olympic Games, does not have an independent international federation recognized by the International Olympic Committee, and will not be on the Olympic Programme anytime soon. It does have the FAI Ballooning Commission within the FAI (Fédération Aeronautiques Internationale).
