Description

Arts competitions were held at the Ancient Olympic Games, with the Competition for Trumpeters (22 times) and Competition for Heralds (18 times) contested most often. All of the other events were on the program only once or twice. The Heralds and Trumpeters events were first held at the 396 BCE Olympics.

Did you know?
