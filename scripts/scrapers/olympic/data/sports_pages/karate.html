Description

Karate is an Oriental martial art that developed primarily on the Japanese island of Okinawa, but quickly spread to China. There are multiple styles and schools of karate but competitive karate is mostly held in the kata and kumite styles. Karate has been held at multiple regional Games and will be on the program of the 2020/21 Tokyo Olympic Games. Karate World Championships have been held since 1970. As a competitive sport, karate is governed by the World Taekwondo Federation, which had 198 member nations as of 2020.
