Predict Host's Situation

This part of the application will allow you to understand the economic situation of a country both, before and after hosting the olympics based on various economic factors.

What does it do? - The predictor takes a country and the year as an input.
- Based on this input, it will then use forecasting to get the country's economic standing in the future, i.e. when it is to be the host.
- Based on these forecasted values, the predictor will predict how the country will do after it hosts the olympics.
- This prediction will be based on several economic factors, the user's decision to select the country to host (or not) shall be aided through these predictions.
