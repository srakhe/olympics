Sentiment Analysis

This part of the application will allow you to view the people's sentiments regarding particular olympic games.

Why is this important? - By hosting the olympics, it is the people living in that country whose lives will be affected.
- Although most of the budget may be privately funded, part the taxpayers money goes into hosting the olympics too.
- There may be other effects of the olympics besides just economic factors and this may be reflected in peoples sentiments.
For example, Tokyo olympics were hosted during the ongoing COVID-19 crisis. This had a negative impact on the local's health and their sentiments can be seen to be negative in this case.
